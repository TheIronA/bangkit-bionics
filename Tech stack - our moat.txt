Tech stack - our moat

Sensors - FMG + EMG 97% accuracy in humid environments compared to 83% for EMG alone (Ke et al., Sensors, 2020 — their custom sensor, not ours yet. We target comparable performance.)

NN - Current plan: ANN (MLP/CNN) via TFLite Micro on nRF52840. SNN is a post-prototype research question — requires neuromorphic hardware (e.g. BrainChip Akida), not compatible with nRF52840. Revisit after Year 2 clinical data.

Socket Comfort - 3D print, reduce drop rate

EEG - ear-EEG starts as third signal source alongside FMG+EMG (68-83% accuracy alone today). Every patient session builds proprietary EEG+EMG+FMG dataset. As data accumulates and models improve, EEG gradually replaces EMG+FMG entirely. Internal R&D — keep off investor materials until Phase 2 hardware exists.
